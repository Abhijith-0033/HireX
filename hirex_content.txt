Motivation
Recruitment platforms receive a high volume of applications for each job
posting, making manual screening inefficient.
Recruiters spend significant time reviewing resumes, leading to delayed
hiring decisions.
Traditional recruitment systems rely heavily on keyword-based filtering,
resulting in poor candidate–job matching.
Skill mismatch between candidates and job requirements reduces hiring
quality.
Manual evaluation processes are prone to bias and inconsistency.
There is a growing need for intelligent systems that automate screening,
ensure fairness, and improve recruitment accuracy.

System Architecture
HireX follows a layered, modular architecture to ensure scalability
and maintainability.
Users access the system through a unified frontend interface.
The frontend communicates with backend services using secure
APIs.
Backend services manage authentication, job aggregation,
applications, and scheduling.
An AI layer performs resume parsing, matching, ranking, and
shortlisting.
A centralized database stores all recruitment-related data.
External systems provide job listings and notification support.
